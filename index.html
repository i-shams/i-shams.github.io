Best places to Visit

Kabul

Kabul is the capital city of Afghanistan. It is located in the heart of the country with a population of more than 8 million people. Kabul is the city of embassies, ministries, and most goevernement facilities.

Badakhshan

Badakhshan is 4th biggest province of Afghanistan located in the northern region. It shares borders with China, Pakistan and Tajikistan. It's beautiful sceneries and nature attracts a lot of tourist.

Herat

Herat is the 3rd largest province of Afghanistan. It is located in the North East region and shares border with Iran. Herat has rich cultural heritage and a lot of historic monuments.

Jalalabad

Jalalabad is located in Ningarhar province and is famous for having good weather throught the year. It's beautiful weather and greenery attracts a lot of people to travel here.
